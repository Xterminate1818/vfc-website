About

Veterans for Christ was formed by a group of Veterans in 2023 to assist in the general welfare and personal healing of the Veteran. Our mission is to help the Veteran redefine their relationship with God in a number of ways:

  • Examine with the Veteran their present relationship with God
  • Help the Veteran to define their ideal relationship with God
  • Walk with the Veteran on their journey to achieve their ideal relationship with God
  • Excersise patience, allowing the Veteran to grow at their own pace on their spiritual journey
